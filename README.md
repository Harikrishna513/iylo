# IYLO Bakehouse — Website Guide

A simple overview of your online bakery for the IYLO Bakehouse team.  
No technical knowledge needed — this explains what the website does for your business and customers.

---

## What this website is

**IYLO Bakehouse** is a contemporary, premium bakehouse in Jayanagar, Bangalore, specialising in modern baking and eggless creations — sweet and savoury.

This website is your digital storefront. Customers can browse the menu, place orders, save favourites, and reach you for gifting, bulk orders, celebration cakes, and workshops. You manage products, orders, and enquiries from a private Admin area.

---

## For your customers

| What they can do | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Browse the full **menu** by category | Easy to find cakes, cookies, breads, desserts, and more |
| View **product details** (description, price, delivery & storage tips) | Clear expectations before ordering |
| Add items to **cart** and checkout | Online ordering without calling for every item |
| Save items to a **wishlist** | Come back later to products they love |
| Create an **account** | Track orders, addresses, and saved items |
| Contact you for **gifting & bulk**, **birthday & wedding cakes**, and **workshops** | High-value enquiries land in one place |
| Find **store location, hours, phone, WhatsApp, and email** | Easy to visit or get in touch |

**Ordering notes customers see on the site**
- Minimum order amount applies
- Free delivery above a set order value
- Pickup available at the bakehouse
- Many items need a short pre-order lead time (typically 1–2 days)

## For you (Admin)

Sign in to the **Admin** area to run day-to-day operations without asking a developer for every change.

### Products (menu)
- Add, edit, or hide products
- Set prices, descriptions, and category
- Mark bestsellers, new items, seasonal, or featured
- Manage sizes / variants and stock where needed
- **Bulk import** from a spreadsheet (CSV) — fill in product **name** and **category**; website links are created automatically (no technical “slug” fields to fill)

### Orders
- See incoming orders
- Update order status as you prepare and deliver

### Customers
- View customer list and related order activity

### Enquiries
- Review corporate / B2B, gifting, cakes, and workshop interest forms submitted from the site

### Settings & team
- Adjust business settings as available in Admin
- Manage who on your team can access Admin

## Everyday tips for the team

1. **Keep the menu up to date** — hide products that are out of season or unavailable so customers don’t order them.
2. **Use clear product names** — the website creates the product page link from the name automatically.
3. **Use category names you already use in the bakehouse** (e.g. Celebration Cakes, Viennoiserie) when importing spreadsheets.
4. **Check enquiries regularly** — corporate and celebration requests often need a personal reply.
5. **Update store hours** when they change, so the website matches the door.
